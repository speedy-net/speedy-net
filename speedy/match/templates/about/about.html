{% blocktrans with site_name=site_name %}Speedy Match is a new dating/matching app for singles who are members of Speedy Net. With Speedy Match you can find matching people for dating and communicate with them online. You can add people as friends on Speedy Net, like people and send them messages. Speedy Match is open to free registration for people from all countries and ages (no age limit!). It is currently available in two languages - English and Hebrew.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans with site_name=site_name %}The founders of Speedy Match are vegan, support animal rights and object to experiments on animals. Speedy Match is a dating app friendly for vegan and vegetarian people and animals.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans with site_name=site_name %}Only people who match your requirements will be able to see your profile and contact you. You can select who will be able to see your profile and which details will appear on your profile as public, which details only to friends and which details will be private. Private details will appear only to you.{% endblocktrans %}
