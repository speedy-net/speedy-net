{% blocktranslate with site_name=site_name %}About Speedy Composer{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate with site_name=site_name %}Speedy Composer will be a new website for composing melodies automatically for members of Speedy Net. It will use the technology of Artificial Neural Networks to compose melodies for you. The melodies you compose will be saved in your personal compositions directory. {% blocktranslate with site_name=site_name %}Speedy Composer is named after Speedy, a female dog that was with us for many years and died in 2003. Speedy gave birth to 7 puppies in 1988.{% endblocktranslate %}
